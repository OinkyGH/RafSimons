Eastpak Padded Pak’r®

Raf Simons × Eastpak

The Definitive Backpack Reimagined

First released in 2008, Raf Simons’ rework of the Eastpak Padded Pak’r® fused subcultural edge with utilitarian design—

featuring oversized metal rings, sleek silhouettes, and a rebellious spirit that defined the collaboration.

About this Collab

The partnership began with the Spring/Summer 2008 “Material World” collection and has since featured New York–inspired leather accents (AW17), Blade Runner–lined PVC bags (SS18), and the punk-patched “Solar Youth” sets (SS20).

Key Items

SS20 Padded Pak’r®

Reimagines the classic backpack with industrial-inspired padded detailing and bold metal hardware, merging utilitarian function with avant-garde aesthetics

SS20 Solar Youth Messenger

Blends sleek utility with rebellious flair, featuring bold graphics and signature metal ring accents
