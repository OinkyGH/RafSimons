Design Philosophy

Simons' work synthesizes:

  • Youth subcultures (punk, rave, mod)
  • Industrial design principles (functionality, minimalism, raw materials)
  • Artistic collaborations (Sterling Ruby, Peter Saville)
  • Deconstruction of traditional tailoring
