Dr. Martens × Raf Simons

Limited-Edition Subculture Staple

In 2017, Raf Simons re-envisioned the iconic 1460 boot with deconstructed tongues, exposed stitching, and platform soles—bridging punk heritage and haute design.

About this Collab

Dr. Martens’ air-cushioned sole has anchored underground style since 1960. Partnering with Raf Simons infused it with minimalist tailoring and bold deconstruction—limited runs that sold out instantly and now command collector prices.

Small batches and exclusive boutiques turned each drop into a cultural event—cementing it as one of the decade’s most celebrated designer collaborations.
